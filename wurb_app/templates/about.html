CloudedBats WURB-2024

Version: {{ wurb_version }}

CloudedBats WURB-2024 is a Do-It-Yourself, DIY, bat detector. It is based on three main parts; a small computer called Raspberry Pi, an ultrasonic microphone, and free and open source software. Then there are some optional parts like GPS dongle, etc.

Note: CloudedBats WURB-2024 is under development. Please use the earlier major version until the new one is finished and well tested. Link to the earlier version: CloudedBats WURB-2020.
